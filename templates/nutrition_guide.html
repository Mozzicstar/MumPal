🍲 Nutrition & Meal Guide

Eat right for your stage — whether pregnant or nursing

🤰 Pregnancy Nutrition

✅ Essential Foods

🥬 Leafy Greens
Spinach, kale, lettuce
Rich in folate and iron
Prevents birth defects

🥛 Dairy Products
Milk, yogurt, cheese
Calcium for bone development
Protein for growth

🥭 Colorful Fruits
Oranges, berries, mangoes
Vitamin C and fiber
Natural sugars for energy

🐟 Lean Proteins
Fish, chicken, beans, eggs
Essential amino acids
Omega-3 for brain development

🌾 Whole Grains
Brown rice, quinoa, oats
B vitamins and fiber
Sustained energy

⚠️ Foods to Limit/Avoid

🚫 High Mercury Fish
Shark, swordfish, king mackerel
Can harm baby's nervous system

🍷 Alcohol
No safe amount during pregnancy
Can cause birth defects

☕ Excessive Caffeine
Limit to 200mg per day
About 1 cup of coffee

🥩 Undercooked Meat
Risk of bacterial infection
Always cook thoroughly

🧀 Unpasteurized Products
Soft cheeses, raw milk
Risk of listeria

🤱 Breastfeeding Nutrition

💡 You need an extra 300-500 calories per day while breastfeeding

💪 Nutrient Boosters

🥑 Healthy Fats
Avocados, nuts, olive oil
Support baby's brain development

🐟 Omega-3 Rich Foods
Salmon, sardines, walnuts
Crucial for baby's vision and brain

🥛 Extra Calcium
Dairy, leafy greens, almonds
Prevent bone loss

💧 Hydration
8-10 glasses of water daily
Essential for milk production

🍖 Iron-Rich Foods
Red meat, spinach, lentils
Prevent maternal anemia
