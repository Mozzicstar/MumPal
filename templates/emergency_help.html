🚨 Quick Actions

⚠️ When to Seek Emergency Help

🤰 During Pregnancy

  • 🔴 Severe abdominal pain
  • 🔴 Heavy bleeding
  • 🔴 Severe headache with vision changes
  • 🔴 Difficulty breathing
  • 🔴 Severe vomiting (can't keep fluids down)
  • 🔴 High fever (over 38°C/100.4°F)
  • 🔴 Decreased fetal movement
  • 🔴 Leaking amniotic fluid
  • 🔴 Severe swelling of face and hands

🤱 After Birth (Postpartum)

  • 🔴 Heavy bleeding (soaking a pad per hour)
  • 🔴 Severe headache
  • 🔴 Difficulty breathing
  • 🔴 Chest pain
  • 🔴 Severe abdominal pain
  • 🔴 Signs of infection (fever, chills)
  • 🔴 Thoughts of self-harm
  • 🔴 Severe mood changes

👶 Baby Emergencies

  • 🔴 Difficulty breathing
  • 🔴 Blue lips or face
  • 🔴 High fever in newborn
  • 🔴 Not waking up or very lethargic
  • 🔴 Persistent crying (over 3 hours)
  • 🔴 Not feeding
  • 🔴 Severe vomiting
  • 🔴 Signs of dehydration
